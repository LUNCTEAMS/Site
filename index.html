FAQ

Quick answers for delegators.

What do I get by delegating?

You support high-uptime validator operations and may qualify for monthly airdrops depending on the program and rules. Proof is posted each cycle.

How often do you run cycles?

Monthly by default: snapshot → burn → airdrop → report. Any schedule change is announced in advance.

What is the 40M rule?

The $USTC airdrop program requires a minimum of 40M $LUNC staked to qualify.

Can you guarantee outcomes?

No one can guarantee outcomes. We focus on consistent execution and transparent proof posted on-chain each cycle.
