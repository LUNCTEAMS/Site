FAQ

Quick answers for delegators.

What do I get by delegating?

You help fund revenue-backed burns and may qualify for monthly airdrops depending on the program and rules. Proof is published each cycle.

Where do burns and airdrops come from?

They’re funded from validator revenue. We publish TX links after each cycle so anyone can verify.

How often do you run cycles?

Monthly by default: snapshot → burn → airdrop → report. Any schedule change is announced in advance.

What is the 40M rule?

The $USTC airdrop program requires a minimum of 40M $LUNC staked to qualify.

Can you guarantee price outcomes?

No one can guarantee outcomes. What we can do is execute consistent, transparent burns and rewards — with proof each cycle.
