FAQ

Quick answers for delegators.

What do I get by delegating?

You help fund revenue-backed burns and you may qualify for monthly airdrops depending on the validator program and rules. Proof is published each cycle with TX links and a report.

Where do burns and airdrops come from?

They’re funded from validator revenue (commission and operational allocations). We publish TX links after each cycle so anyone can verify.

How often do you run cycles?

Monthly by default: snapshot → burn → airdrop → report. Any schedule change is announced in advance.

What is the 40M rule?

The $USTC airdrop program on Kraken Node requires a minimum of 40M $LUNC staked to qualify.

Can you guarantee price outcomes or a repeg?

No one can guarantee outcomes. What we can do is execute consistent, transparent burns and rewards — and publish proof on-chain every cycle.
